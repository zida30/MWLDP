Rabia Khalid

MWLDP Program Coordinator

Rabia Khalid (she/her)

  • The MWLDP Program Coordinator at the Brooklyn College Women's Center leads the Muslim Women's Leadership Development Project, creating programs and events that support Muslim women students. They collaborate with local organizations, campus clubs, and professionals to provide resources and mentorship. The role includes event planning, budgeting, and ensuring all events use halal vendors.
Rabia.Khalid@brooklyn.cuny.edu

Biography

Rabia Khalid is the program coordinator of the Muslim Women’s Leadership Development Project (MWLDP). She earned her bachelor's degree in psychology in 2024, driven by a passion for understanding and supporting others. Rabia joined the Women’s Center as a Federal Work Study student in Fall 2022 and has worked there for two years. She is deeply passionate about expanding the reach of MWLDP, making it more visible on campus, and advocating for Muslim women. Rabia strives to create a space where Muslim women can find support, resources, and empowerment. As she pursues her master’s in Human Resources, she is committed to helping students, faculty, and the broader community. Her vision is to ensure MWLDP becomes a cornerstone for Muslim women’s growth, addressing their unique challenges and fostering a strong, connected community.

What Do I Love About MWLDP?

  • I love how MWLDP provides a supportive space for Muslim women to connect, grow, and lead with confidence. Being part of a team that is dedicated to empowering and uplifting our community is truly inspiring, and I can't wait to see the incredible things we'll accomplish together this year. /li>
