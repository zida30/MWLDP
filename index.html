What is MWLDP?

The Muslim Women’s Leadership Development Project at the Brooklyn College Women’s Center provides representation for Muslim women in various career fields, fostering a mentorship network and a supportive community. The initiative offers a safe space for Muslim students and all students, hosting meaningful events each semester to address concerns such as Islamophobia and religious accommodations. Additionally, the project collaborates with the Women’s Center to organize a variety of events. The center is located at Brooklyn College, Ingersoll Extension 227.


The Muslim Women’s Leadership Development Project also addresses issues related to gender, mental health, and career growth, offering resources and support in these key areas. We aim to empower Muslim women by providing mentorship and guidance on navigating these challenges while fostering an inclusive environment. Our focus on community-building and holistic support ensures that students feel connected and encouraged in both their personal and professional development. Through these efforts, we strive to create a lasting impact on the lives of all students involved.
